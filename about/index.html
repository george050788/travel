About me｜因為走過同樣的路，所以我更懂你在意什麼

18年我從台灣移民到美國，我不只適應文化與語言，入行貸款行業多年，仔細研究過美國金融制度的規則與細節。

在不同體系之間轉換的過程中，我深刻體會到，對多數新移民而言，真正困難的從來不是申請貸款本身，而是如何在資訊不對稱與制度差異下，做出正確決策。

市場上從不缺方案，缺的是判斷。也正因如此，我在評估每一個案件時，看的不只是條件是否能核准，而是整體方案是否真正符合客戶的長期規劃與財務安全。

這段經歷，讓我比多數人更清楚——新移民在貸款決策時，真正需要的不是更多選項，而是一個能看懂全局的人。

很多人以為貸款只是利率問題。但在實務中，真正影響成本的往往不是利率，而是誠信。

我看過不少案例：客戶一開始看到的是看似理想的利率，但在交易即將完成時，條件卻出現變化，付出的代價，總是藏在文件裡。

也因此，我始終認為——利率只是表面，真誠才是關鍵。對我來說，利率只是方案的一部分；但即使是其中一部分，我也會確保它具有市場競爭力。

About me｜Because I have walked this path myself, I understand what truly matters to you.

I immigrated from Taiwan to the U.S. in 2018. Beyond adapting to culture and language, I spent years in mortgage lending studying the rules and details of the U.S. financial system.

Through navigating different systems, I learned that for many new immigrants, the hardest part is not the mortgage application itself—it is making the right decision when information is uneven and systems are unfamiliar.

The market has many loan programs. What people lack is clear judgment. That is why when I review a file, I do not only ask whether it can be approved—I ask whether the structure supports your long-term financial safety and goals.

This experience gives me a perspective many others do not have: new immigrants do not need more options—they need someone who can see the full picture.

Many believe mortgage decisions are just about rate. In practice, cost is often affected less by the headline rate and more by honesty and execution.

I have seen clients start with an attractive rate quote, only to face changing terms near closing. The true cost is often hidden in the documents.

That is why I believe rate is only the surface—integrity is the foundation. Rate still matters, and I make sure your terms stay competitive in the real market.
